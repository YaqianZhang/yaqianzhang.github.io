Enmei Tu (屠恩美)

Short Bio

I am an assistant professor at the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering (SEIEE), Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I obtained PhD degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China in 2014. Before joining SJTU, I worked as a postdoc Research Fellow at Auckland University of Technology, New Zealand and Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, From Jan. 2015 to Dec. 2017.

Research Interests

Machine Learning , Nonlinear Manifold Learning , Semi-supervised Learning , Data Mining , Neural Information Processing
